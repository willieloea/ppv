About

Mission

Pierewiet Property Valuations is a property valuation firm located in Windhoek, Namibia. The company was established in 2006 with the vision to provide reliable property valuations to its customers, by using relevant data, while utilizing years of experience.

We want our clients to be able to rely on our integrity and professionalism to trust our recommendations.

Staff

Pierewiet Property Valuations staff consists of 7 members: 1 Principal valuer (Owner), 5 Associate valuers (Qualified), and 1 Admin clerk/student.
Pierewiet Property Valuations is a fully Namibian owned entity, with 83 years of collective experience in the formal property valuation sector of Namibia.
We have service level agreements with the following Banks: FNB since 2006, Nedbank since 2006, Bank Windhoek since 2006, Standard Bank since 2017, Bank of Namibia, Development Bank of Namibia and Agricultural Bank of Namibia
Some of our private clients include University of Namibia, Road Contracting Company, Nampower, Rundu Mall, Mega Centre, Woermann & Brock, Nexus, amongst others.

Services

We can perform the following types of valuations for private clients and commercial banks, throughout Namibia:

  • Residential properties valuation
  • Commercial properties valuation
  • Industrial properties valuation
  • Farms and lodges valuation
  • For insurance purposes (complexes and any other properties)
  • Development valuations (i.e. townships, complexes, shopping malls, etc.)
  • Off plan valuations
  • Progress valuations on building projects
  • Expert witness in court
